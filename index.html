Dalgona coffee recipe

This yummy South Korean coffee that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard - you feel like you've won the jackpot, right?

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Hot water
  • Instant coffee powder or Expresso Granules
  • Milk

Dalgona making process

Step 1:Combine the instant coffee, sugar and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 tablespoons of hot water to a medium mixing bowls.
Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy
Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed
Step 3: Pour milk and garnish with coffee cream
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
